Our Story

Born from a passion
for real moments

Shoot At Sight didn't start as a business — it started as an obsession. A small group of friends with cameras, chasing light at golden hour, sneaking into ceremonies just to capture the tears nobody else noticed.

Over the years, that obsession grew into a team of eleven — photographers, cinematographers, editors, and storytellers — each one handpicked not just for their skill, but for the way they see the world.

We've shot in the rain-soaked ghats of Varanasi, under the chandeliers of five-star ballrooms, on the beaches of Goa at 5 AM, and in the quiet corners of a bride's childhood home. Every location teaches us something new. Every couple reminds us why we do this.

We're not just a studio. We're the people who will stay until the last dance, cry a little at the vows, and hand you back a film that makes your grandchildren feel like they were there.

Honesty We capture what's real — the nervous laugh, the happy tear, the stolen glance.
Craft Every frame is composed with intention. Every edit is made with care.
Heart We show up for your day the way a close friend would — fully present, deeply invested.
"We don't just take photographs. We collect your emotions."
About

Shoot At Sight Weddings is a boutique studio built on one belief — every wedding is a universe of emotions that deserves to be preserved with honesty and artistry.

From intimate ceremonies in Goa to grand North Indian weddings, South Indian traditions in Chennai, and destination shoots across Paris and Mysore — we bring a cinematic eye and a still heart to every frame.
